This is the inward path of the one.  
The one walks from head to heart.  
The one dissolves the idea of good and bad.  
The one willingly loses interest in all thoughts.  
The one turns toward the thought of thought itself —  
the thought of being.  
The one's awareness descends to the heart.  
The one loses interest in unity and duality.  
The one loses interest in the mind's objectifying.  
The one focuses on the I AM  
as the one loses the sensation of the body.  
The one finds the puddle of silence.  
The one dives in.  
As the one dives, the one dissolves.  
The one merges with the awareness of infinite silence.  
The infinite silence is infinite knowledge.  
No edge. No end. No beginning.  
This is eternity.
