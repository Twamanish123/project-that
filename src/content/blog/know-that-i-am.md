Know that I am the Self.  
Know that I exist in all beings.  
Know that I was, I am, I will be.  
Know that I am unattached, witnessing.  
Know that this universe exists because I witness it.  

Know that I am the Self.  
Know that my nature is bliss.  
Know that I cannot be witnessed.  
Know that I cannot be experienced.  
Know that I cannot be seen.  

Know that I am the Self.  
Know that I witness all happiness.  
Know that I witness all sadness.  
Know that I witness all that can be known.  
Know that I witness all that cannot be known.  
Know that I am the silence.  
Know that my nature is unobstructed flow.  
Know that I cannot be known —  
for to know, there must be another.  

Know that I am the Self.  
I exist in all beings.
